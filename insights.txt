Things to keep in mind:

1-Reports and Analytics:

Provide users with insights through task-related reports and analytics to track productivity and completion rates.

2-Dark Mode:

Offer a dark mode option for users who prefer a darker interface.

3-Implement an activity log to track changes and updates made to tasks.

4-Allow users to archive or move completed tasks to maintain a clean task list.

5-Implement a search feature to help users quickly find specific tasks.
